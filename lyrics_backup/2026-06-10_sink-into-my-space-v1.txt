[Hook]
Close the door and let the shadows fall
Leave the heavy world outside the wall
Sink into my space and cross the line
(Cross the line)
Tonight the only magic is yours and mine

[Verse 1]
Kick your boots off by the cedar door
I’ve been craving this since yesterday
Hear the rain against the wooden floor
Let the rush outside just wash away

[Pre-Chorus]
Every look you give is a spark in the dark
(A spark in the dark)
Every breath you take is leaving a mark
We’re miles from the noise and the fading light
Just drifting away in the velvet night

[Chorus]
So come a little closer to the flame
(Closer to the flame)
Whisper every secret you never say
Sink into my space, let the dawn just wait
I want to feel the fire that we make

[Post-Chorus]
Sink into my space and take your time
Tonight the only magic is yours and mine

[Verse 2]
